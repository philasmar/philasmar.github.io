For an opioid addict, finding personalized resources for medication can be challenging. From our research and patient interviews, we found that the current system is complicated. We are building an application that will help people navigate the system and ensure a good path to recovery.
Nopioid is an app that customizes care information based on the patient. With an intuitive interface, the patient and their care providers have information they need. No more looking around! We have trained machine learning algorithms on patient data to create a personalized guide for the patients. Nopioid provides information for every stage of the process - medication, detox, rehab, treatment plan, and sober living.
We will be offering Nopioid on Android, iOS and Web.
Nopioid is a semester long project, it may or may not ever be available.
